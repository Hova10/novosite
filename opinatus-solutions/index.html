About us

Designing a Website is like a way of telling a story, powered by imagination and crafted by creativity that has zero limits

Step 1 - Plan

We will work with you to reveal your site objectives, who your target market are and uncover all the relevant information we need to market your business effectively online. A sitemap will be created in this stage showing you all the pages on your site and how your information will be structured.

Step 2 - Design

A user interface will be created from the information in the planning stage. Our designers will then move on to create a fresh look and feel for your website looking at things like colours and imagery. This is an iterative process and we try and make sure you are 100% happy before we go into build.

Step 3 - Build

Our trained website developers all build to W3C standards using clean code and semantic markup. We can offer a range of accessibility levels which you can choose from. Our content management system will usually be integrated into your website for you to easily update content.

Step 4 - Manage

Our e-marketing specialist will work with you to track users and discover what is working and what isn't. We can determine what search terms are used and give you advice on how to get your site to the top of the search engines. It is important to keep your website up-to-date and fresh which is where we can help.
