CASE STUDY

For this project i had to create a facebook app with a timeline, where users could share their stories with their own VW van.

I tried to maintain a sober look, which is a signature mark of VW itself, but also wanted to keep things fun for VW users.

RESULT

The app itself was a success with a large number of users sharing their own stories and pictures of their Vintage Vans.

It fulfilled the original purpose of the app but it also allowed for VW users to meet other VW users and set up meetings around the country.
